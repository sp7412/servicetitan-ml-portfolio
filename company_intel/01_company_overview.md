# 01 — Company Overview

## What They Are

ServiceTitan is the dominant vertical SaaS platform for home and commercial service
contractors — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, pest control, landscaping, and
more. They position themselves as the **"operating system for the trades."**

Founded 2007 by Ara Mahdessian and Vahe Kuzoyan. Cloud platform launched 2012.
Headquartered in Glendale, CA. IPO'd December 12, 2024 on Nasdaq as `TTAN`.
Global team of 3,000+ employees ("Titans").

---

## Founding Story (Know This)

Both founders grew up watching their fathers run trade businesses on pen and paper.
Mahdessian's father ran a plumbing business; Kuzoyan's ran HVAC. This origin story
is deeply embedded in culture and repeated at every conference — it explains *why*
the company is building what it's building and why customer obsession is genuine,
not marketing speak.

## What They Sell

Three revenue layers:

### Core Platform (~71% of revenue — subscription)
- **CRM** — customer records, job history, equipment tracking
- **Scheduling & Dispatch** — drag-and-drop dispatch board, tech assignment
- **Invoicing & Estimates** — price book, estimate builder, e-signatures
- **Accounting** — QuickBooks/Intacct integration, payroll, GL
- **Mobile App** — offline-capable iOS/Android app for field technicians
- **Reporting & Analytics** — real-time dashboards, KPI tracking

### Pro Products (premium add-ons, drives expansion revenue)
| Product | What it Does |
|---|---|
| Marketing Pro | SMS/email campaigns, review automation, ad attribution |
| Phones Pro | Call recording, transcription, analytics |
| Scheduling Pro | Online booking widget, AI voice/SMS booking agents |
| Dispatch Pro | AI-assisted technician assignment, job value prediction |
| Contact Center Pro | Full AI-powered omnichannel contact center |
| Sales Pro / Field Pro | AI coaching for technicians in the field |
| Pricebook Pro | Price benchmarking, configurable services, vendor matching |
| Fleet Pro | GPS tracking, risky driver detection |

### FinTech (~25% of revenue — usage-based)
- ServiceTitan Payments (card processing on jobs)
- Customer financing (third-party at point of sale)
- Affirm BNPL integration (launched Sept 2025)
- Take rate: ~0.25% of Gross Transaction Volume

## Competitive Landscape

ServiceTitan targets the **mid-market to enterprise** tier (contractors doing $2M–$50M+ ARR):

- **SMB threats**: Jobber (~$50/mo), HouseCall Pro (~$65/mo) — explicit non-target
- **Same tier**: FieldEdge, Workwave, ServiceTrade, BuildOps, AccuLynx, JobNimbus
- **Enterprise/horizontal**: Salesforce Field Service, SAP FSM, ServiceMax

**Moat**: Very high switching cost once CRM, price book, job history, and accounting
are all in ServiceTitan. Combined with >95% gross dollar retention.
